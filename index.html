🚕 Getting Around MateraLeave the car at the hotel every evening — all three restaurants are under 6 min walk. No ride apps operate in Matera. For the Belvedere viewpoint, ask hotel reception to call a local radio taxi (~€8–10). The ZTL zone covers most of the centro storico.

Matera Essentials

  • Park the car — the center is fully walkable, cars cannot enter the Sassi.
  • The Sassi are best at golden hour (7–8pm) and dawn — dramatically lit and very quiet.
  • Wear proper shoes — the stone streets are steep and uneven throughout.
  • Local DOP specialties to try: crapiata (ancient grain soup), cruschi peppers, Matera bread.
  • Primitivo is the local wine — earthy, powerful, excellent value.
